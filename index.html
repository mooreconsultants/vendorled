FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a real estate licence to use Vendorled? +
You need to hold a valid real estate licence or be onboarded in partnership with a licensed professional. Vendorled is designed to work within Australian property law. We verify your credentials during onboarding.
How does the AI voice agent sound? +
Natural. The AI uses advanced voice synthesis with sub-500ms response latency — the industry benchmark for human-like conversation. You can listen to real call recordings before you commit.
Can I still list on REA and Domain? +
Yes. Vendorled generates a fully formatted, portal-ready listing package for REA and Domain that takes under 5 minutes to upload. We also integrate with listing aggregators for automated distribution.
What happens to my buyer data? +
You own it entirely. All lead data, call recordings, and contact details belong to your organisation and are stored securely in Australia. You can export everything at any time.
How quickly does the AI respond to a new enquiry? +
Within 60 seconds of a lead submitting an enquiry — day or night. Compare that to the industry average of 4+ hours for agent follow-up.
What's the difference between Vendorled and 360AI? +
360AI handles post-enquiry nurture only — SMS and email follow-up after a lead already exists. Vendorled owns the entire funnel: we generate your website, run your ads, call your leads the moment they enquire, and book the meeting. 360AI has no website generation, no listing syndication, and no voice calling capability.
When will I get access? +
We're launching with a South Australia pilot cohort first. Join the waitlist and you'll be notified when your region opens.
